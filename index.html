What is Health Coaching?

Health coaching is a powerful tool for increasing accountability and helping you achieve your health goals. By working with a coach, you can develop the skills and strategies you need to stay on track and make progress towards a healthier and happier life.

Emotional Eating

Emotional eating is a common issue with negative health repercussions. Health coaches assist by identifying triggers like stress, educating on healthy coping mechanisms, fostering self-awareness of emotional patterns, and offering emotional support to develop positive changes and provide affirmations.

Chronic Stress

Chronic stress can harm your health, but health coaching offers ways to manage it. Coaches identify triggers, create coping strategies, guide lifestyle changes, promote a positive mindset, and provide ongoing support for stress reduction. Health and wellness coaching can empower you to enhance your overall well-being by addressing chronic stress.

Accountability

Health coaching significantly enhances accountability by clarifying goals, helping to creating action plans, offering regular check-ins, providing support and encouragement, and celebrating successes. With a coach's guidance, you can develop the skills and strategies necessary to achieve your health goals and maintain commitment to a healthier life.

Weight Loss

Health coaching simplifies weight loss with personalization, expert guidance, behavior modification, education, and support for emotional well-being. We take a holistic approach, addressing overall health alongside weight loss. Remember, a coach complements medical advice, and consulting a healthcare professional is crucial for underlying health concerns.

About Me

Hello, I'm Katherine Craig, and I've been where you might be right now. I used to feel like life was passing me by, dealing with chronic stress, disordered eating, and unhealthy food habits. Exercise was the last thing on my mind, and I was constantly anxious. My goal each day was simply to get through it. But today, I wake up with joy, excited about the day ahead.

My relationship with food is healthy, and I've overcome my eating disorder. Chronic stress is a thing of the past, and my perspective on life has completely transformed.
